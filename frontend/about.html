E-Sumbong Kay Kap!

Building a better Barangay through connection, action, and care.

About the Project

The E-Sumbong Kay Kap! system connects residents of Barangay Pulong Buhangin directly with their barangay officials. It ensures that reports, complaints, and suggestions are acted upon efficiently — bridging the gap between citizens and local governance.

Our Mission

To empower citizens through transparency and technology — ensuring every concern is heard and addressed with accountability.

Our Vision

To build a responsive and connected barangay community where collaboration drives progress and safety.
